## SITUATIONAL ANALYSIS

**Sky States** is an online ed-tech training platform (skystates.us) offering courses in:
- Data Science & AI
- Cyber Security & Ethical Hacking
- DevOps & Cloud Computing

**Key challenge:** AI Overviews, search results, and third-party forums surface concerns about:
- Aggressive sales/enrollment tactics
- Unclear refund policies
- Upselling pressure
- Lack of verified student outcome data
- Transparency gaps

**Why SkyReviews.us is the right vehicle:**
- You control it
- It's already affiliated (can be disclosed, not hidden)
- Adding content to it bypasses the WordPress access issue on skystates.us
- It can rank independently for "[Sky States review]" queries
- It can serve as the trust/transparency hub that skystates.us currently lacks

**Core principle:** Do not suppress criticism. Publish the truth, with context and data. Credibility comes from honesty.

### 2.3 Review Methodology

**URL:** `/transparency/methodology`

**Structure:**
```
H1: How SkyReviews.us Reviews Are Created

H2: Our 7-Factor Review Framework
  1. Curriculum Quality (20 points)
     - Industry relevance, tool coverage, project work, updates frequency
  2. Instructor Credentials (15 points)
     - Real-world experience, teaching background, availability
  3. Pricing Transparency (15 points)
     - All-in cost clarity, hidden fee audit, comparison to market
  4. Refund & Cancellation Policy (15 points)
     - Fairness, clarity, actual enforcement
  5. Career Support (15 points)
     - Job placement services, alumni network, employer connections
  6. Student Experience (10 points)
     - Platform usability, support responsiveness, community
  7. Outcome Verification (10 points)
     - Verifiable placement data, salary data, graduate tracking

H2: Data Sources We Use
  - Student interviews (minimum 3 per program)
  - Public review platforms (Google, Trustpilot, Reddit)
  - Curriculum audit (actual access to course materials where possible)
  - LinkedIn graduate tracking
  - Published outcome reports from the company

H2: What We Do Not Do
  - Accept payment for positive reviews
  - Allow reviewed companies to edit content before publication
  - Remove negative reviews at company request
  - Fabricate or anonymize student quotes without consent

H2: Update Schedule
  All company profiles reviewed every 6 months or when material
  changes occur (pricing, policy, leadership changes).
```

### 2.5 Student Verification Process

**URL:** `/transparency/verification`

**Structure:**
```
H1: How We Verify Student Reviews

H2: Why Verification Matters
  Unverified reviews — positive or negative — can mislead prospective
  students. We apply a tiered verification system.

H2: Verification Tiers

  TIER 1: VERIFIED ENROLLED STUDENT ✓
  - Student provides proof of enrollment (receipt, course access
    screenshot, enrollment email)
  - Review marked with "Verified Enrolled" badge
  - Highest trust level

  TIER 2: VERIFIED GRADUATE ✓✓
  - Student provides proof of completion (certificate, transcript,
    completion email)
  - Review marked with "Verified Graduate" badge

  TIER 3: UNVERIFIED REVIEW
  - Published with "Unverified" label
  - Treated with lower weight in editorial scoring

H2: What We Check For
  - Duplicate accounts (IP/email matching)
  - Review farms (identical language, bulk submission timing)
  - Incentivized reviews (we ask: were you offered anything for
    this review?)
  - Company-planted reviews

H2: Appeals Process
  If you believe a review about you is fake, submit a challenge
  via [form link]. We investigate within 10 business days.
```
